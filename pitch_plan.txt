# **2. Our Solution: QUBO-RAG**

### **2.1 The Key Idea**

* Formulate retrieval as a **Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization (QUBO)** problem.
* The selection of diverse, relevant documents becomes an **Ising Hamiltonian**.

This allows:

* **Global optimisation** (not greedy)
* **Diversity penalty + relevance reward** in a single objective
* **Provable optimality under annealing**

### **2.2 Why Quantum Dice Hardware Is Needed**

Frame this strongly:

* The search space grows exponentially with K.
* Classical solvers choke (combinatorial explosion).
* Probabilistic/annealing hardware provides **massive parallel exploration**.
* We exploit native Ising architecture → **natural fit for the hardware.**

A slide comparing runtimes:

* Greedy MMR: fast but low quality
* Classical QUBO solver: accurate but slow
* **Quantum Dice QUBO**: accurate *and* fast
